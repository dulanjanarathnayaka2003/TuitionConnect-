Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring platform

To register as a tutor:

  • Visit our "Tutor Register" page
  • Complete the application form with your details
  • Upload required documents (qualifications, ID, etc.)
  • Select the subjects and levels you want to teach
  • Set your availability and hourly rate
  • Submit for verification

Our team typically reviews applications within 2-3 business days.

Students can easily find tutors through our platform:

  • Search by subject - Filter tutors by specific subjects or topics
  • Location-based - Find tutors nearby or available for online sessions
  • Ratings & reviews - See feedback from other students
  • Availability - Filter by time slots that work for them

Students can view tutor profiles, qualifications, and teaching styles before booking.

Our pricing structure is transparent:

  • For students: No signup or membership fees - you only pay for the sessions you book
  • For tutors: No upfront costs - we take a small commission (15-20%) from each completed session

Payment processing fees may apply depending on your payment method.
